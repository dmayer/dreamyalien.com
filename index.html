About

Professional background

Daniel is a cybersecurity executive and Chief Information Security Officer at Morningstar, where he leads global security across a $2.5B enterprise spanning financial services, data, and technology. A 2025 ChicagoCISO ORBIE Award winner, he has spent his career translating deep technical roots into practical enterprise leadership — building programs, advising boards, and helping organizations move quickly without losing sight of resilience.

Outside his operating role, Daniel invests in emerging security companies through SVCI (Silicon Valley CISO Investments) and serves as a strategic adviser to OFFENSAI, an autonomous cloud-focused breach and attack simulation startup. He brings the same perspective to both — a practitioner’s understanding of what security teams actually need, and a conviction that the best security companies are built in close partnership with the people who use them.
